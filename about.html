About Moahamed

Mochopz embarked on his barbering journey during the pandemic, seeking a meaningful way to spend his time. Starting by offering haircuts to friends and family in his garage, he dedicated himself to mastering the art of haircutting. As he gained confidence, he began offering his services to the youth at his local mosque, further refining his skills. With a growing passion for hairstyling, Mochopz received praise from loved ones, who enthusiastically supported his decision to pursue a professional career in barbering.
